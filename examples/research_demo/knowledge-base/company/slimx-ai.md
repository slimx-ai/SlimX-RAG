# SlimX.ai Research Demo

SlimX.ai builds vendor-neutral AI software and engineering systems for sovereign RAG, controlled agents, and robotics-facing edge AI.

The strongest business wedge for research organizations is controlled AI infrastructure: private knowledge, tool-using workflows, evaluation, documentation, and deployment control across EU cloud, on-prem, and local environments.

SlimX emphasizes explicit execution, inspectable behavior, and deployable architecture rather than opaque chatbot demos.
